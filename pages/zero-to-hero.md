# Zero to Hero

"*Zero to Hero*" is an upcoming, PvP-centered YouTube series. I will both interview and participate in 1v1 sessions with some of the best in each class, and go over some of the quirks of the most played builds, and learn how to play against them. Not every holy grail will be shown, as this would be too unfair for everyone. But it will follow a progress of getting a zero (me) to hero and into platinum.
